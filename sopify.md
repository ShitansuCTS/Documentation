# Razorpay Integration on Shopify

## Project Title
Razorpay Payment Gateway Integration for a Fresh Shopify Store

---

## 1. Overview
This document explains the approach, rationale, and high-level steps
for integrating **Razorpay** as the payment gateway on a **fresh Shopify store**.
It is intended for internal documentation and management review.

---

## 2. Purpose
- To standardize payment gateway setup for Shopify projects
- To ensure a scalable, reliable, and investor-friendly payment solution
- To document the integration flow for future reference and onboarding

---

## 3. Platform Details
- **E-commerce Platform:** Shopify (Fresh Store)
- **Payment Gateway:** Razorpay
- **Primary Region:** India
- **Additional Support:** International card payments

---

## 4. Why Razorpay
Razorpay is selected due to the following advantages:

- Trusted and widely adopted in India
- Native integration with Shopify
- Supports multiple payment methods:
  - Credit & Debit Cards (Indian & International)
  - UPI
  - Net Banking
  - Wallets
- Strong dashboard for:
  - Transaction tracking
  - Refund management
  - Settlement reports
- Scalable for future needs such as:
  - Subscriptions
  - APIs
  - Advanced analytics
- Positive perception among partners and investors

---

## 5. High-Level Integration Flow

### Step 1: Razorpay Account Setup
- Create a Razorpay business account
- Submit business details
- Complete KYC verification
- Add and verify bank account

**Estimated Time:** 1–3 working days

---

### Step 2: Shopify Store Setup
- Create or use a fresh Shopify store
- Configure basic store settings:
  - Store currency
  - Business information

---

### Step 3: Razorpay Integration in Shopify
- Navigate to:




- Select **Razorpay** as the payment provider
- Enter Razorpay credentials:
- Key ID
- Key Secret
- Activate the payment gateway

**Integration Time:** Same day

---

### Step 4: Payment Configuration
- Enable required payment methods in Razorpay Dashboard:
- Cards
- UPI
- Net Banking
- Enable international payments if required
- Configure settlement and payout preferences

---

### Step 5: Testing
- Use Razorpay **Test Mode**
- Perform test transactions to validate:
- Successful payments
- Failed transactions
- Refund workflow

---

### Step 6: Go Live
- Switch Razorpay account to **Live Mode**
- Monitor initial transactions
- Verify settlements and payouts

---

## 6. Benefits of This Setup
